The Mrs. Carter Show World Tour is the upcoming fourth concert tour by American recording artist Beyoncé Knowles. The tour's title is a reference to her marriage to Shawn "Jay-Z" Carter.[1]

[edit] Background

The tour was announced following Knowles' performance at the Super Bowl XLVII halftime show on February 3, 2013. Initial dates in Europe and North America were announced, with more to be announced in Latin America, Australia and Asia.[2] With little promotion, tour stops in Los Angeles, Las Vegas, San Jose, Miami, Dallas, Houston, Chicago, Detroit, Boston, D.C. and Brooklyn sold out in minutes. [3]
