Portsmouth F.C. in European football

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Portsmouth F.C.'s record in Europe was in the 2008–09 UEFA Cup where they played Vitória Guimarães and Braga of Portugal, Italian team A.C. Milan and Heerenveen of the Netherlands.
