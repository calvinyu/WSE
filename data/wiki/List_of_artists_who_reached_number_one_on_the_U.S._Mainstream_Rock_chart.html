List of artists who reached number one on the U.S. Mainstream Rock chart

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
This article does not cite any references or sources. Please help improve this article by adding citations to reliable sources. Unsourced material may be challenged and removed. (December 2010)

This is an alphabetical list of artists who reached the number one position on the United States Billboard Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart. The number in parentheses indicates the total of number-one hits for that band on this chart. The chart has been active since 1981.
