This "Billboard Hot 100 Best Charting Artists" list of best-charting music artists in the United States includes acts from anywhere in the world who have charted singles on the Billboard Hot 100 since the beginning of the rock and roll era (1955).

In addition to the U.S. Hot 100, the table includes (in the U.S. only):

  • Top 40 hits
  • Top 10 hits
  • Hot Adult Contemporary Tracks ("AC")
  • Hot Adult Top 40 Tracks
  • Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks
  • Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs
  • Hot Country Songs
  • Billboard 200 LPs (excludes greatest hits, compilation, live, Christmas, and soundtrack albums)

For records outside the U.S., the table includes:

  • UK singles
  • Canada singles
  • Australia singles
