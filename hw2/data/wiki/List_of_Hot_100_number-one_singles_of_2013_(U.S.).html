List of Hot 100 number-one singles of 2013 (U.S.)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The Billboard Hot 100 is a chart that ranks the best-performing singles of the United States. Its data, published by Billboard magazine and compiled by Nielsen SoundScan, is based collectively on each single's weekly physical and digital sales, as well as airplay.
